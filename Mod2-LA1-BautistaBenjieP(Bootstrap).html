Green tea is good for your health

For thousands of years, tea has been used for medical purposes in China and Japan. Green tea is promoted as a beverage and dietary supplement for

  • increasing mental alertness
  • alleviating digestive symptoms and headaches
  • reducing bad breath.

Green tea can also cause negative effects such as anxiety, tremors, impatience, and sleeping issues.
